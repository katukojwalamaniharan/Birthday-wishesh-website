## How to Copy and Use

1. Download or fork this repo.
2. Open the project folder.
3. Replace the images inside **assets/** with your own (keep same file names for fastest setup).
4. Open `index.html` and change the text / name / date.
5. (optional) tweak colors & fonts in `styles.css`.
6. Deploy anywhere (GitHub Pages / Netlify / Vercel) and share your link.
